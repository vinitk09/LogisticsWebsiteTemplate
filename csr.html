Carca Rapid Solutions(CRS), formerly known as Carca Road Transport Pvt Ltd, is the flagship company and core strength of Carca Group Founded in 1979, headquartered in Jamshedpur in Jharkhand,India. The company started operations with transportation of consignments in eastern and north eastern region in India.Carca World Logistics is one of the pioneers of the industry. At a time when transshipment was considered to be the norm within the surface transport industry, Carca World Logistics started with its specialization with a “point-to-point” service model with a promise of 50% reduction in stock delivery time, the company grew manifold. Today, Carca World Logistics is present over 350 locations across the nation, and has a fleet of own vehicles and a nationwide network.This step helped Carca to strengthen its customer base and expand into a pan-india organization.

The company also boast to have one of the best people in the industry. Today Carca Group is highly diversified and has presence in different business models in services,manufacturing and trading in the industry.
