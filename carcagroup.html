Bharatiyam Foods

A part of Carca Group, Bharatiyam Foods is an OEM manufacturer for Hindustan Coco-Cola Beverages Pvt Ltd. At a fully-automated state-of-the-art facility with the latest 600 BPM and 500 JPH units, Bharatiyam foods manufacture, package and distribute its drinking water brand ‘Kinley’ in several states of India.

Spread over 16 acres, the bottling unit is based out of Bidadi, Bangalore. The plant operates day & night, all round the year. With 600 BPM unit in 2015 as compared to 120 BPM unit in 2014, the unit is deemed as the best performing plant in terms of safety, quality, efficiency and eco-friendliness.
